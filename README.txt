== Description ==

Autumn here ? Add nice looking animated falling leaves to your Wordpress site and enjoy autumn.

Attract your visitors with these realistic looking nice falling leaves and they will love not just your content but also your seasonal design.

No configuration needed. Just install and activate. That's all.

For more information take a look at the [video preview](http://www.wpmaniax.com/seasonal-plugins/)


== Installation == 

1. Drop the 'autumn-falling-leaves' folder into your 'wp-content/plugins' folder
 
2. Go to your Administration: Plugins page and activate the "Autumn Falling Leaves" plugin
